What HyFlex Is

Flexible Participation

Our HyFlex (Hybrid-Flexible) courses provide flexible participation options for modern adult education students with responsibilities that require alternatives to traditional learning schedules.

Learning Options

These classes are provided by experienced instructors across our partner counties (Clay, Knox, Laurel, and Whitley) with 1.5 hour lessons on math, language arts, science, or social studies topics. Times and days are varied to accommodate the maximum number of students.

About Our Programs

All adult education services are free of charge. We provide classes, tests to help determine what you should study, study materials (online and hard copy), and practice testing. The only thing students might have to pay for are official GED exams after passing practice (Ready) tests.

The regular cost of these tests is $30-36 each. However, Kentucky students can often take advantage of statewide promotions that reduce or waive fees altogether for a first attempt at tests, making it free! Test scholarships are also available through donations of those who support our mission.
