What is HyFlex?

The HyFlex Carousel is...

  • a flexible learning program
  • made for adults with busy schedules
  • designed for you to "hop on" or "hop off" at any time
  • a series of 45-minute lessons over math, reading, science, and/or social studies
  • taught by experienced teachers in our partner counties of Clay, Knox, Laurel, and Whitley
  • offered morning, afternoon, and evening so you can choose what's best for you

How the Carousel Works:

  • on a 4-week cycle, like a ride that loops around
  • each week covers different skills...
  • but you won't feel behind if you start in the middle
  • we offer lessons mainly at two levels: Foundational (beginner) & Intermediate (in the middle)
  • move up when your instructor thinks you're ready
  • built to help you succeed at your own pace

About Our Programs

All adult education services are free of charge. We provide classes, tests to help determine what you should study, study materials (online and hard copy), and practice testing. The only thing students might have to pay for are official GED exams after passing official practice tests (GED Ready).

The regular cost of these tests is $36 each. However, Kentucky students can often take advantage of statewide promotions that reduce or waive fees altogether for a first attempt at tests, making it free! Test scholarships are also available through donations of those who support our mission.
